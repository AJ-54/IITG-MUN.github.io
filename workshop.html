IIT Guwahati Model United Nations brings to you, for the very first time, a two-day MUN workshop! This unprecented opportunity is the perfect place for amateurs as well as seasoned delegates to groom themselves for the upcoming flagship conference. Whether yor are just looking for the hang of an MUN, to refine your debating skills, or to explore your potential in an extemporary environment, this workshop is one initiative that caters to all these needs and aspires to give you personalised guidance. This is your one chance to participate and learn from the best. Highly experienced executive board members of distinguised MUNs shall serve as your mentors. So, do join us on the first and second of this September to aquire your cutting edge as a delegate!
